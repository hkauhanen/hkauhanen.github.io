hipster

byte / R / 5 July 2020
#r

A.k.a. Henri’s Idiosyncratic Package of Somewhat Tedious Everyday Routines. Implements a handful of R functions I wish were part of the default installation.

Status

Very much in research and development.

Installation

With devtools:

devtools::install_github("hkauhanen/hipster")

Usage

Documentation is less than comprehensive at the moment, but you may consult the manual – most of the routines are quite self-explanatory.
